Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how this website collects, uses, and protects your information. This site was created as part of a university web development project. It includes a restaurant digital transformation case study and interactive features such as a cookie consent system.

1. Information We Collect

This website does not collect personal data such as names, emails, or contact details.

However, we may collect non-personal information such as:

  • Basic analytics (e.g., page visits, browser type)
  • Device or usage data
  • Cookie preferences stored in your browser

2. Cookies and Tracking

This website uses cookies to improve the experience and allow certain features to function properly. Cookies are small files stored on your device.

We use two types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies – needed for the site to work (e.g., saving cookie preferences).
  • Performance cookies – optional cookies that help us understand how the site is used.

3. Your Cookie Choices

When you visit the site, a cookie banner appears. You can choose:

  • Accept All Cookies – allows all cookie types.
  • Manage Preferences – allows selective control.

Your choice is saved locally in your browser and can be cleared at any time by removing the cookieChoice value in your localStorage.

4. Third-Party Content

The restaurant case study may include third-party integrations such as embedded maps or links to external websites. These external sites have their own privacy policies and are not controlled by us.

5. Restaurant Case Study (Digital Transformation)

The “Restaurant Digital Transformation” page demonstrates how a traditional restaurant might use simple website features to improve online visibility — such as menus, booking information, opening hours, social media links, and contact forms.

This is fictional and for educational purposes only. No real business data is collected.

6. How We Protect Your Data

No personal data is collected or processed by this website. All cookie choices are stored only on your device.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or this project, you can contact me through my university email.
